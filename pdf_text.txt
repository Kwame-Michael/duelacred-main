DUELA TECH| MVP Business Model — Working Capital Financing  
 MVP  Business  Model
Working Capital Financing for Retail and Inventory-Based Businesses in Botswana
A baker buys flour, butter, and packaging for BWP 2,000 every Monday. By Friday she has sold everything for
BWP 2,800. She made BWP 800 profit. But if she only has BWP 800 in her account on Monday she can only
buy BWP 800 of ingredients , which means she can only bake and sell BWP 1,120 worth of goods. Her 
business is not failing. It is constrained by the cash available at the start of each cycle. Duela Tech gives her 
the BWP 2,000 she needs to operate at full capacity.This document defines the Duela Tech MVP business model — specifically how working capital financing 
works, which businesses qualify, how funds are disbursed and protected, and how repayments are collected. It
is the definitive operational reference for the first phase of the platform.
1. WHAT IS WORKING CAPITAL FINANCING
1.1 Explanation
Working capital is the money a business needs to operate before its revenue comes in. Most retail and 
inventory businesses face a timing gap — they have to spend money buying stock before they can earn money
selling it. This gap is what Duela Cred finances.
1.2 Why This Works as a Financing Product
The money is tied to a specific, verifiable purpose — buying stock. Not a vague business goal.
The repayment comes from a real, existing revenue stream — the business is already selling and already
generating cash

vetting approach, and disbursement method.DuelaCred finances retail and inventory-based businesses in Botswana that meet three criteria: they are
already generating consistent revenue, they have a specific and verifiable stock-buying need, and they have
the repayment capacity to service the campaign comfortably from additional revenue generated.The cash cycle is short — stock is bought, sold, and revenue collected within days to weeks, not months
The risk is not whether the business can generate revenue — it already is. The question is whether the
working capital unlocks enough additional revenue to cover the repayment comfortably

5. THE GRADUATED LIMIT MODEL

--- PAGE 12 ---
Every SME on DuelaCred starts at a capped first-campaign limit regardless of how large their stated need is.
Access to larger campaigns is earned through demonstrated repayment performance — not granted upfront
based on promises.
The graduated limit system is communicated to every SME at the point of application — not as a restriction but
as a pathway. The graduated limit model serves two purposes simultaneously. It protects investors by limiting exposure to
unproven SMEs. And it creates the most powerful repayment incentive available — the SME has a direct
commercial reason to repay on time because doing so unlocks more capital for their next campaign.
